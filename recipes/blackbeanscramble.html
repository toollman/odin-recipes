Mexican Black Bean Scrambled Eggs

Description

Tired of the same old scrambled eggs? Then this recipe is for you! Spice up those scramble eggs with some black beans and top it off with salsa!

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans drained and rinsed
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 small onion diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mexican spices to taste
  • 2oz portabella mushrooms diced
  • 4 slices of toast
  • Salsa of choice as topping (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil over medium high heat
  2. Saute onions and jalapeno until fragrant
  3. Add black beans and stir until heated through
  4. Crack eggs into beans and onion mixture along with spices and stir to scramble eggs
  5. Remove from heat and divide into 4 equal servings and place on top of toast
  6. (optional) Top with salsa and enjoy
